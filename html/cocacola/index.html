Recycle Vending-Machine

When used plastic bottles or aluminum cans are placed in the Coca-Cola recycling bins, $0.05 each will be donated to Special Olympics. When we combined the two 'values' of helping the environment and being able to donate, many people started to participate with interest. Although the program started as a pilot, it will be installed in more places in the future to raise awareness and participation in recycling.

Society

The Coca-Cola Foundation's Journey to a Better World

The Coca-Cola Foundation, which has grown into a global charitable organization over the past 30 years, has donated approximately $1 billion (approximately KRW 1.11 trillion) for the sustainable development of communities around the world. Coca-Cola keeps its promise to return 1% of the previous year's operating profit to society every year, and provides financial support to the Coca-Cola Foundation through donations. In 2017, Coca-Cola and the Coca-Cola Foundation donated $138 million to more than 330 organizations in more than 110 countries around the world. The process of solving the various problems that the local community is experiencing has been a history of challenges, opportunities, and evolution for the Coca-Cola Foundation. Since 2007, support activities conducted inside and outside the classroom have gradually expanded to various fields such as global water conservation programs, community programs, and recycling programs. The foundation's activities are in line with the company's sustainable management policy and are meaningful in that they also encompass women's rights and entrepreneurship.

Water and Environmental Program Support

The Coca-Cola Foundation is working to improve access to clean water and sanitation. While protecting areas with deteriorated water quality, we are conducting educational programs for community members so that they can continue to use clean water. In 2017, more than $5.6 million was invested in North America to create recycling bins and various recycling programs.

Empowering Women

It is also committed to strengthening the economic empowerment of women. For example, the Coca-Cola Foundation is partnering with international NGOs to develop and implement programs to help women in developing countries learn computer and financial knowledge, land rights and business skills. It also provides funding for a variety of services, including housing, childcare, medical care, and transportation as needed.

Community Reinforcement

The Coca-Cola Foundation works to strengthen and enrich communities through support for youth education, arts and cultural programs, HIV/AIDS treatment and prevention, drug delivery, and disaster relief.
